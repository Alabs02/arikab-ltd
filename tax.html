Tax

Tax

We don't just ensure your tax compliance; we strategically engineer your tax posture to become a significant value driver, aggressively maximizing your benefits and rigorously defending your interests in an increasingly complex and scrutinized fiscal world.

Our job is to ensure clients comply with tax regulations and also take advantage of adjustments , deductions and credits that will benefit the clients' businesses. We work with our customers to design tax plans that are appropriate for the tax system of the future as tax authorities and the general public continue to review the tax strategies of multinationals and domestic businesses. We can handle both your accounting and tax functions. We can help you to organise your financial hitches with your banks and tax officials.

As tax consultants, our tax services are categorised under two major categories:

  • Corporate Tax Management

  • Personal Income (PAYE) Tax Management

Corporate Tax Management

Our Corporate Tax Management service covers Company Income Tax (CTI), Petroleum Tax, Education Tax, Value Added Tax (VAT) and Witholding Tax.

Personal Income Tax

This involves structuring the employees' payroll in the most tax efficient manner. As tax consultants, we will file annual returns of income tax and processing of TCC on behalf of Executive Directors of the company and staffs.

Tax Compliance Services include:

  • Value Added Tax and Customs Compliance

  • Witholding Tax Compliance

  • Tax Health Check

  • Tax Dispute Resolution

  • Payroll and Human Resource Services

  • Preparation and Filings of Tax Returns

  • Obtaining Tax Clearance Certificates

  • General Advice on Compliance Matters

  • Audit Support
